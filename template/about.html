About Scrap-Maker

Turning old into gold, one product at a time.

Our Mission

Scrap-Maker is built to encourage sustainability by giving second-hand products a new life. We connect buyers and sellers on a trusted platform where affordability meets responsibility.

How It Works

  • List your old but working items for sale.
  • Browse categories to discover what you need.
  • Buy & sell securely with easy payment integration.

Why Choose Scrap-Maker?

  • Affordable alternatives to new products.
  • User-friendly design with light & dark modes.
  • Secure transactions and trusted sellers.
  • Support eco-friendly practices by reducing waste.

At Scrap-Maker, we believe one persons scrap can be another person treasure.
